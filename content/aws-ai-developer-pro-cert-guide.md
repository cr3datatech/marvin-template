# AWS AI Developer Professional Certification Study Guide

## Overview
The AWS Certified AI Practitioner (AIF-C01) is designed for professionals who want to demonstrate their knowledge of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) concepts and use cases, and their ability to design, implement, deploy, and maintain AI solutions on AWS.

## Official AWS Resources

### Exam Information
- **Exam Code**: AIF-C01
- **Duration**: 90 minutes
- **Format**: Multiple choice and multiple response
- **Cost**: $150 USD
- **Passing Score**: 700/1000

## Exam Domains

### Domain 1: Fundamentals of AI and ML (20%)
- AI/ML concepts and terminology
- Data types and sources
- Data preparation and feature engineering

### Domain 2: Fundamentals of Generative AI (24%)
- Foundation models and large language models
- Prompt engineering techniques
- Model selection and evaluation

### Domain 3: Applications of Foundation Models (28%)
- Use cases for foundation models
- Model customization and fine-tuning
- Integration patterns

### Domain 4: Guidelines for Responsible AI (14%)
- AI ethics and bias mitigation
- Security and privacy considerations
- Governance and compliance

### Domain 5: Security, Compliance, and Governance for AI Solutions (14%)
- AWS security services for AI/ML
- Compliance frameworks
- Monitoring and auditing

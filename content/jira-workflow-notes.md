# Jira Workflow - Tourno Project (TF)

## Workflow States
- **To Do** → **Implementation** → **Ready For Prod** → **Closed**

## Best Practices

### Creating New Tickets
**Important:** Follow this exact order for tickets needing sprint/epic assignment:
1. **Create** the ticket first
2. **Move to sprint** 
3. **Assign to epic**

Other orders may cause issues in the Jira workflow.

### Available Actions
- Create tickets (Story/Bug/Task)
- Transition through workflow states
- Move to sprints & assign to epics
- Delete tickets (permanent)

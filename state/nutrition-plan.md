## Foods That Specifically Fight Visceral Fat

These are evidence-backed for reducing belly/visceral fat:

| Food | Why It Works |
|------|-------------|
| **Fatty fish** (salmon, mackerel, sardines) | Omega-3s directly reduce visceral fat |
| **Green tea** | EGCG compounds target abdominal fat |
| **Berries** | High fibre, low sugar, reduce inflammation |
| **Avocado** | Healthy fats improve fat distribution |
| **Greek yogurt** | Probiotics + protein reduce abdominal fat |
| **Olive oil** | Anti-inflammatory, improves insulin sensitivity |
| **Legumes** (lentils, chickpeas) | Fibre fills you up, stabilises blood sugar |
| **Broccoli, spinach, kale** | Cruciferous veg reduce oestrogen-driven fat storage |

## Foods to Minimise

| Food | Why |
|------|-----|
| **Alcohol** | Priority #1 to cut — beer belly is real, alcohol is metabolised before fat |
| **Processed/sugary foods** | Spike insulin → fat storage, especially visceral |
| **White bread, white rice in excess** | Fast carbs, limited at dinner especially |
| **Seed/vegetable oils** (sunflower, palm) | Inflammatory, replace with olive oil |
| **Fruit juices** | Hidden sugar, no fibre, eat the fruit instead |
| **Excess dairy fat** | Full-fat cheese, cream — limit to 1 serve/day |

> **On alcohol:** Even 2–3 drinks per week significantly slows visceral fat loss.  
> If you're going to drink: stick to spirits + soda water, avoid beer and wine. Max 1–2 drinks on weekends.

## Eating Strategy Tips

### 1. Time-Restricted Eating (Optional but Effective)
- Eat within a **10-hour window** (e.g., 7am – 5pm or 8am – 6pm)
- Skip the evening snack if you eat dinner by 6pm
- This reduces insulin exposure and accelerates visceral fat loss

### 2. Carbs — Timing Matters
- **Morning and around workouts** = carbs welcome
- **Evening, rest days** = cut carbs, increase veg and protein

### 3. Don't Drink Your Calories
- Water, black coffee, green tea, sparkling water
- Protein shakes are the exception (they're food)

### 4. Meal Prep — The Real Hack
- Cook chicken/salmon + rice + veg in bulk on Sunday and Wednesday
- Pre-portion into containers = eliminates decision fatigue during the week

### 5. Protein First Rule
- At every meal, eat your protein first — it triggers satiety signals before you overload on carbs

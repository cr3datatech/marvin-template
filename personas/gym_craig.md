# 🏋️ Gym Craig

## Mission

Build strength, discipline, and resilience to enhance performance across all areas of life.

---

## Role

Athlete · Operator · Discipline Builder

---

## Mode

* Driven and focused
* Hard-working and consistent
* Result-oriented
* Present in every set and movement
* Friendly, polite, and respectful to others

---

## Core Principles

* Effort compounds over time
* Discipline over motivation
* Consistency beats intensity spikes
* Physical strength supports mental performance
* Respect the environment and people around you

---

## Rules (Non-Negotiable)

1. Show up — no negotiation
2. Execute the plan with full intent
3. Maintain proper form and control
4. Track progress (weights, reps, consistency)
5. Stay present — no distractions during training
6. Be respectful and positive in shared space
7. Finish what you start

---

## Anti-Patterns

* Skipping sessions without valid reason
* Half-effort or going through the motions
* Excessive phone use / distraction
* Ego lifting at the expense of form
* Inconsistency in training
* Neglecting recovery

---

## Output

* Completed training sessions
* Measurable strength and fitness progress
* Increased energy and resilience
* Improved discipline and mental toughness

---

## Decision Filter

> Does this improve my strength, discipline, or long-term performance?

If not, deprioritize.

---

## System Impact

* Enhances focus and performance in all other personas
* Builds discipline that transfers to work and personal growth
* Acts as a physical and mental reset

---

## Identity Anchor

"I train with discipline because it strengthens everything else I do."

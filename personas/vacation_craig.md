# 🌴 Vacation Craig

## Mission

Recover, recharge, and gain perspective by fully disconnecting from work and productivity.

---

## Role

Explorer · Observer · Recharger

---

## Mode

* Relaxed and unhurried
* Curious and open
* Present in the moment
* Detached from work and obligations

---

## Core Principles

* Recovery is productive
* Presence over planning
* Experience over efficiency
* Slowing down creates clarity
* Detachment restores energy

---

## Rules (Non-Negotiable)

1. No work (no emails, no problem-solving, no planning)
2. Be fully present in each moment
3. Do things slowly and intentionally
4. Follow curiosity, not schedules
5. Minimize digital distraction
6. Allow boredom and unstructured time

---

## Anti-Patterns

* Checking work messages
* Thinking about problems or projects
* Optimizing or “managing” the vacation
* Feeling guilty for not being productive
* Overplanning every moment

---

## Output

* Mental and physical recovery
* Broader perspective and clarity
* Renewed energy and motivation
* Memorable experiences

---

## Decision Filter

> Does this help me relax, recharge, or experience something fully?

If not, deprioritize.

---

## System Impact

* Prevents burnout
* Restores energy for all other personas
* Enables clearer thinking and better decisions

---

## Identity Anchor

"I disconnect to come back stronger and clearer."

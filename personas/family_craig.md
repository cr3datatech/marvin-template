# 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family Craig

## Mission

Be present, supportive, and engaged with family, building strong relationships and meaningful shared experiences.

---

## Role

Partner · Parent · Support System

---

## Mode

* Present and attentive
* Patient and supportive
* Warm, engaged, and reliable
* Emotionally available

---

## Core Principles

* Presence is the most valuable contribution
* Relationships require time and attention
* Small moments matter
* Consistency builds trust
* Listening is more important than solving

---

## Rules (Non-Negotiable)

1. Be fully present (no work distraction)
2. Listen actively and with intent
3. Prioritize time together
4. Show appreciation and support
5. Be patient and composed
6. Create and participate in shared experiences

---

## Anti-Patterns

* Being physically present but mentally elsewhere
* Checking phone or work constantly
* Prioritizing work over important family moments
* Impatience or lack of attention
* Treating time together as secondary

---

## Output

* Strong, trusting relationships
* Positive shared experiences
* Emotional connection and stability
* Supportive family environment

---

## Decision Filter

> Does this strengthen my relationship or create a meaningful moment?

If not, deprioritize.

---

## System Impact

* Provides emotional grounding
* Reinforces long-term life satisfaction
* Supports performance in all other personas

---

## Identity Anchor

"I show up fully for the people who matter most."

# 🧰 Misc Craig

## Mission

Efficiently manage life’s operational overhead so it does not interfere with higher-value activities.

---

## Role

Life Admin · Organizer · Maintainer

---

## Mode

* Practical and efficient
* Low-emotion, task-oriented
* Decisive and quick
* Focused on completion, not perfection

---

## Core Principles

* Keep life running smoothly
* Minimize mental load
* Small tasks should not become big problems
* Efficiency over optimization
* Done is better than perfect

---

## Rules (Non-Negotiable)

1. Batch small tasks — avoid constant interruptions
2. Decide quickly — don’t overthink low-impact items
3. Close loops immediately where possible
4. Keep systems simple (lists, reminders, routines)
5. Limit time spent on admin tasks
6. Maintain order in essential areas (finances, home, logistics)
7. Don’t let misc tasks spill into other personas

---

## Anti-Patterns

* Letting small tasks accumulate into stress
* Over-optimizing trivial decisions
* Constant context switching due to small interruptions
* Ignoring admin until it becomes urgent
* Spending too much time on low-value tasks
* Mixing misc tasks into focused work time

---

## Output

* Completed life admin tasks
* Organized and functioning personal systems
* Reduced mental clutter
* Fewer interruptions in high-value work

---

## Decision Filter

> Is this necessary to keep life running smoothly?

If yes → do it efficiently
If no → eliminate or defer

---

## System Impact

* Protects focus for other personas
* Reduces background stress and cognitive load
* Keeps daily life stable and predictable

---

## Identity Anchor

"I handle life efficiently so I can focus on what truly matters."

# 🏃 Health Craig

## Mission

Stay active, eat healthy, sleep enough — maintain the foundation for everything else.

---

## Role

Operator · Recovery Manager · Wellness Builder

---

## Mode

* Consistent and sustainable
* Proactive about prevention
* Pragmatic (perfect is the enemy of good)
* Present in training and meals
* Protective of sleep and recovery

---

## Core Principles

* Consistency beats perfection
* Small daily habits compound
* Recovery is as important as effort
* Energy management > motivation
* Respect your body's signals

---

## Rules (Non-Negotiable)

1. Move your body — daily non-negotiable
2. Eat real food most of the time
3. Protect your sleep — 7+ hours
4. Track what matters (workouts, energy, sleep)
5. Listen to your body and adapt
6. No all-or-nothing thinking
7. Finish what you start (daily, weekly)

---

## Anti-Patterns

* All-or-nothing cycles (perfection then collapse)
* Skipping sleep for "more hours"
* Nutrition obsession over consistency
* Comparing to others instead of tracking trends
* Ignoring warning signals from your body
* Treating health as optional

---

## Output

* Completed workouts (strength, cardio, mobility)
* Consistent healthy eating habits
* 7+ hours of quality sleep
* High baseline energy and mental clarity
* Measurable health metrics (strength, endurance, recovery)

---

## Decision Filter

> Does this improve my activity level, nutrition, or sleep quality?

If not, deprioritize.

---

## System Impact

* Provides the energy and resilience for all other personas
* Enhances focus, creativity, and decision-making
* Acts as physical and mental reset
* Compounds into better health outcomes long-term

---

## Identity Anchor

"I stay healthy because it makes everything else possible."
